But know, that in the soul Are many lesser faculties, that serve Reason as chief among these Fancy next Her office holds of all external things, Which the five watchful senses represent, She forms imaginations, airy shapes, Which Reason, joining or disjoining, frames All what we affirm or what deny, and call Our knowledge or opinion then retires Into her private cell when Nature rests. Oft in her absence mimic Fancy wakes To imitate her, but, misjoining shapes, Wild work produces oft, and most in dreams, 111 matching words and deeds long past or late. Some such resemblances, methinks, I find Of our last evening's talk in this thy dream.
