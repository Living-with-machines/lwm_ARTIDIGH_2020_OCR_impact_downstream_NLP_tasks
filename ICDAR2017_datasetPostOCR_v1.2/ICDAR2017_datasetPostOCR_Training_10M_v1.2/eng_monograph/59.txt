To hem have I so great affeccioun, As I seyde erst, whan comen is the May, That, in my bed ther daweth me no day, That I nam up and walkyng in the mede, To seen this floure ayein the sonne sprede, Whan it up ryseth erly by the morwe That blisful sight softeneth al my sorwe, So glad am I, whan that I have presence Of it, to doon it alle reverence.
